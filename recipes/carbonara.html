Carbonara

Description

This recipe is one of the most popular classical italian pasta to make wherever you are, because of its simplicity and tastyness.

Ingredients

For 2 people

  • 100g of guanciale
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 50g of pecorino or grana padano
  • 10g of salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. First of all we put water to heat up untill it boils.
  2. Meanwhile the water is heating up we cook the guanciale in a pan with medium heat untill it's crunchy.
  3. Once the water is boiling we put the pasta to cook following the instructions in it's packaging, with half a tablespoon of salt.
  4. We put the eggs and the grated cheese in a bowl and we mix it together with black pepper.
  5. The guanciale should be done by now, so we mix it's grease with the eggs, cheese and pepper (that's gonna give the dish a punch of flavor).
  6. When the pasta is cooked we separate it from the water and we add it to the bowl while it's still hot, mixing it for a while untill it's integrated with the sauce.
  7. With all these ingredients mixed up it's time to put them in the plate and add some cheese and the guanciale on top to finish with our recipe.
